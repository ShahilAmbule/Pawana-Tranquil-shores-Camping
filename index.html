About Tranquil Shores Camping


Welcome to Tranquil Shores Camping, your gateway to a serene Pawana Lake experience. Nestled amidst nature, our campgrounds offer a perfect retreat from the bustle of city life. Immerse yourself in the beauty of moonlit nights and the gentle lapping of the lake's waters.

Our camping packages are designed to provide you with an unforgettable experience. From lakeside tents to moonlight camping, each option promises a unique adventure.

"Welcome to Tranquil Shores Camping, where tranquility meets adventure. Nestled by the serene Pawana Lake, our campground offers an escape into nature's embrace. Immerse yourself in the tranquil beauty of the lakeside view while indulging in a variety of activities. Unwind in our chill areas, bask in the peaceful ambiance, or engage in our dedicated playing zones. Discover the perfect blend of relaxation and recreation at our lakeside haven.


Discover our specially curated playing areas where both young and young-at-heart can engage in various activities, from friendly games to adventurous pursuits. Feel the pulse of excitement at every corner, as you unwind in our designated chill-out zones, designed for relaxation and socializing.

Experience the magic of the great outdoors while basking in the serene atmosphere that blankets our campground. Whether you're seeking a moment of solitude or a delightful gathering with fellow campers, our site offers the ideal space for both.


Join us for an escape into nature's embrace, where the beauty of the surroundings amplifies the joy of shared moments and the simplicity of tranquility. Book your stay and let the lakeside serenity be the canvas for your unforgettable camping adventure."
